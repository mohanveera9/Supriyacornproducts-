Global Export and Import

Supriya Corn Products Pvt Ltd is a prominent player in the global market, exporting high-quality corn products to over 15 countries. Our strategic location near major Indian seaports ensures efficient and timely delivery of products worldwide. We specialize in corn grits, corn flour, and various spices, with a focus on maintaining the highest quality and sustainability standards. Our import operations support our diverse product range, enhancing our ability to meet customer needs and adapt to market demands.

  • Exporting premium corn products and spices to over 15 countries
  • Efficient logistics and timely shipments from major Indian seaports
  • Diverse product offerings including corn grits, corn flour, and spices
  • Commitment to quality and sustainability in all operations
